Cebu's Mountain spots

Cebu is home to several mountains that excite even the most avid hikers, up to those who want to try it for the first time.

Mountain Spot in Cebu

MT. MANUNGGAL PARK

Fondly tagged as the “Little Baguio of Cebu,” Baguio de Cebu Eco Mountain Adventures offers the same excitement and aura of the Summer Capital of the Philippines.

ADVENTURE CAFE

For the travel junkies and thrill seekers, Adventure Cafe and Zipline should be on your bucket list when in Cebu. More so, they claim to be the first resto-adventure in the Philippines.

BUWAKAN NI ALEJANDRA

Flowers of different kinds and of different colors made this flower garden exceptional and trending. If you’re up for the ‘gram game, this is perfect!
